Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

At NX-ORG, we build tools for engineers who value precision and privacy. This policy outlines our commitment to your data security across all our products, including the Green Timer & Stopwatch extension.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All information generated by our tools stays strictly within your local environment.

  • No account registration required.
  • No tracking pixels or telemetry.
  • No background data harvesting.

2. Local Storage Engine

All your practice history, problem notes, and custom settings are stored locally using your browser's internal storage (IndexedDB/Chrome Storage). You have absolute control over this data.

  • You can view your data at any time.
  • You can export your data for backup.
  • You can permanently delete all data via the "Factory Reset" option.

3. Third-Party Access

Since we do not collect your data, we have nothing to sell, trade, or share with third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics services.

4. Open Source Transparency

Our code is open-source and licensed under GPLv3. This allows the community to audit our architecture and verify our privacy claims at any time via our GitHub repositories.

5. Contact

If you have questions regarding our privacy practices, you may reach our system administrators at:

Email: support@nxorg.xchip.in
